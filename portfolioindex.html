Political Theory · 17-page paper
In-depth
Argues that predictive policing undermines liberal commitments to autonomy, due process, and neutrality by operationalizing structural injustice through data and algorithms. Draws on Rawls, Raz, Iris Marion Young, Kymlicka, and Parekh.
